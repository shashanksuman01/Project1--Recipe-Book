Spicy Beef Tacos

Flavorful ground beef tacos with fresh toppings and spices

30 minutes
Serves 6
Easy
Back to Main Courses

Ingredients

For the Beef:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste

For Assembly:

  • 12 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes.
  2. Add diced onion to the beef and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  6. Pour in beef broth and simmer for 5-10 minutes until sauce thickens and flavors meld together.
  7. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed. Remove from heat.
  8. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over gas flame until soft and pliable.
  9. Fill each tortilla with the spiced beef mixture.
  10. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, avocado, red onion, and cilantro as desired.
  11. Serve with lime wedges and hot sauce on the side.

Chef's Tips

  • Use 80/20 ground beef for the best flavor and texture - don't go too lean.
  • Don't skip browning the meat properly - this develops rich flavors.
  • Warm tortillas make a huge difference in the final taco experience.
  • Set up a taco bar with all toppings so everyone can customize their tacos.
