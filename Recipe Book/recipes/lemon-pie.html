Fresh Lemon Pie

Tangy and sweet lemon pie with a buttery crust and meringue

120 minutes
Serves 8
Medium
Back to Desserts

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water

For the Lemon Filling:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons butter

For the Meringue:

  • 3 large egg whites
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 6 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Make the crust: Mix flour and salt in a bowl. Cut in cold butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle ice water over mixture and toss until dough forms.
  2. Roll out dough and press into a 9-inch pie pan. Trim edges and crimp. Prick bottom with fork.
  3. Bake crust at 425째F (220째C) for 10-12 minutes until lightly golden. Remove and reduce oven to 350째F (175째C).
  4. For filling: In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually stir in water until smooth.
  5. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils. Boil for 1 minute.
  6. In a small bowl, lightly beat egg yolks. Gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of the hot sugar mixture.
  7. Return egg mixture to saucepan and bring to a gentle boil. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  8. Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice, zest, and butter until smooth. Pour into baked pie crust.
  9. For meringue: Beat egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar, beating until stiff, glossy peaks form. Beat in vanilla.
  10. Spread meringue over hot filling, sealing edges to crust to prevent shrinking.
  11. Bake for 12-15 minutes until meringue is golden brown. Cool completely before serving, about 3 hours.

Chef's Tips

  • Use fresh lemon juice for the best flavor - bottled juice doesn't compare.
  • Make sure meringue touches the crust edges to prevent shrinking.
  • Cook the filling until properly thickened to avoid a runny pie.
  • Let pie cool completely before slicing for clean cuts.
