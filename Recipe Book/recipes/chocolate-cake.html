Decadent Chocolate Cake

Rich, moist chocolate cake perfect for special occasions

90 minutes
Serves 12
Hard
Back to Desserts

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup strong black coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Frosting:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch round cake pans and dust with cocoa powder.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat eggs, then whisk in buttermilk, coffee, oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined. Don't overmix.
  5. Divide batter evenly between prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then turn out onto wire racks to cool completely.
  7. For frosting: Beat butter until light and fluffy. Gradually add cocoa powder, then alternately add powdered sugar and milk, beating until smooth.
  8. Add vanilla extract and beat until frosting is spreadable. Add more milk if needed for consistency.
  9. Place one cake layer on serving plate. Spread frosting on top, then place second layer on top.
  10. Frost the top and sides of the cake. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving to set the frosting.

Chef's Tips

  • Coffee enhances the chocolate flavor - don't worry, you won't taste the coffee.
  • Room temperature ingredients mix more easily and create a better texture.
  • Don't overbake - the cake should be moist and tender.
  • Let the cake cool completely before frosting to prevent melting.
