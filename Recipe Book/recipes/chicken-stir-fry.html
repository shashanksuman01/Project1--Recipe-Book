Asian Chicken Stir Fry

Quick and healthy stir fry with fresh vegetables and tender chicken

20 minutes
Serves 4
Medium
Back to Main Courses

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced thin
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium broccoli head, cut into florets
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch. Set aside for the sauce.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat until smoking.
  3. Add sliced chicken to the wok and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until golden and almost cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  4. Add remaining oil to the wok. Add onions and stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add bell peppers and carrots, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until vegetables start to soften but remain crisp.
  6. Add broccoli florets and stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  7. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  8. Return chicken to the wok and add the prepared sauce. Toss everything together for 1-2 minutes until sauce coats everything and chicken is fully cooked.
  9. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds.
  10. Serve immediately over steamed jasmine rice.

Chef's Tips

  • Keep the heat high throughout cooking for the best stir-fry texture and flavor.
  • Cut all ingredients before you start cooking - stir-frying happens very quickly.
  • Don't overcrowd the wok - cook in batches if necessary.
  • Vegetables should retain some crunch for the best texture and nutrition.
